# Store Settings

Configure your store's behavior and preferences.

## Accessing Settings

Go to **Admin > Settings** to access all configuration options.

Settings are organized into tabs:
- General
- Appearance
- Shipping
- Inventory
- Domain (Pro)
- Guides

## General Settings

### Store Information

- **Store Name** - Displayed in header and emails
- **Store Email** - Contact email for customers
- **Store Phone** - Optional, shown in footer
- **Store Address** - Business address for footer

### Social Links

Add links to your social profiles:
- Instagram
- Twitter/X
- Facebook
- TikTok
- YouTube

Links appear in your store footer.

## Appearance

### Theme Selection

Choose from available theme presets:
- **Starter tier:** 3 themes
- **Pro tier:** 6 themes

See [Themes](/docs/features/themes) for details.

### Brand Color

Your primary accent color for:
- Buttons
- Links
- Active states
- Highlights

Enter a hex code or use the color picker.

### Logo

Upload your store logo:
- Displayed in header and emails
- Recommended: PNG with transparency
- Size: 200x50 pixels or larger

## Shipping Settings

### Enable Shipping

Toggle to collect shipping addresses at checkout.

When disabled:
- No address collection (for digital-only stores)
- Faster checkout

### Shipping Countries

Select which countries you ship to:
- Customers outside these countries cannot checkout
- Common: US, Canada, UK, Australia

### Shipping Rates

Currently, GoSovereign uses free shipping or flat-rate pricing baked into product prices.

For calculated rates, use Stripe's shipping rate feature.

## Inventory Settings

### Hide Out of Stock

When enabled:
- Products with 0 inventory don't appear in store
- Prevents customer frustration
- Products return when restocked

### Low Stock Threshold

Set when to receive low stock alerts:
- Default: 5 units
- Adjust based on your reorder time

### Low Stock Emails

Toggle to receive email alerts when products hit the threshold.

## Domain Settings (Pro)

See [Custom Domains](/docs/features/domains) for setup instructions.

### Add Domain

1. Enter your domain (e.g., `shop.yourbrand.com`)
2. Click **Add**
3. Configure DNS
4. Verify connection

### Domain Status

- **Pending** - Waiting for DNS verification
- **Connected** - Domain is working
- **Error** - Check DNS configuration

## Guides Tab

Access helpful resources:
- Custom domain DNS guide (downloadable)
- Shipping configuration tips
- Common questions

## Saving Changes

Click **Save** after making changes. Most settings take effect immediately.

Some changes (like domain) require verification steps.

## Tips

### Regular Review

Review settings quarterly:
- Update contact info
- Refresh social links
- Check shipping countries

### Test After Changes

After changing settings:
- View your store as a customer
- Test checkout flow
- Verify emails look correct

### Mobile Check

Settings affect mobile display too. Check your store on phone after changes.
