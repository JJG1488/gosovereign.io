# Digital Products

Sell downloadable files like ebooks, courses, templates, and software.

## Overview

Digital products are delivered automatically after purchase:

1. Customer buys product
2. Payment is confirmed
3. Unique download link is generated
4. Link is sent via email
5. Customer downloads the file

No shipping, no inventory to track.

## Creating a Digital Product

1. Go to **Admin > Products**
2. Click **Add Product**
3. Toggle **Digital Product** on
4. Fill in product details
5. Upload the downloadable file
6. Save

## Upload Limits

- **Maximum file size:** 100MB
- **Supported formats:** Any file type

For larger files (courses, software):
- Split into multiple smaller files
- Use a ZIP archive
- Host externally and provide access instructions

## Delivery

### Automatic Delivery

After purchase:

1. Order confirmation email includes download button
2. Customer clicks to download
3. Secure, signed URL ensures only buyer can access

### Download Limits

- **3 downloads** per order
- **7 days** to download

After limits are reached, customer must contact you for a new link.

### Secure Links

Each download link is:
- Unique to the order
- Time-limited
- Cannot be shared (won't work for others)

## Customer Experience

### Order Confirmation

The email includes:
- Order details
- **Download Now** button
- Link expiration notice

### My Downloads

If customer accounts are enabled, customers can access downloads from:
1. **Account > Orders**
2. Click the order
3. Click **Download** for each digital item

## Mixed Orders

You can sell digital and physical products together:

- Digital items: Delivered immediately
- Physical items: Require shipping

The order confirmation separates:
- "Your downloads are ready"
- "These items will be shipped"

## Pricing Digital Products

No shipping costs means higher margins. Consider:

- **Perceived value** - What would customer pay for this information?
- **Time saved** - How much time does your product save?
- **Competitor pricing** - What do similar products cost?
- **Bundle options** - Offer collections at a discount

## Tips

### Preview Content
Offer a free sample or preview to build trust.

### Clear Descriptions
Explain exactly what's included:
- Number of files
- File formats
- Page count / duration
- System requirements

### Delivery Instructions
Include a README or getting-started guide in your ZIP file.

### Support
Provide clear contact info for download issues.
