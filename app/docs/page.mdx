# Welcome to GoSovereign

GoSovereign lets you create your own private e-commerce store for a one-time price. No subscriptions. No code. No landlords.

## What You Get

When you purchase a GoSovereign store, you get a fully-featured e-commerce platform that includes:

- **Product Management** - Add unlimited products with variants, images, and inventory tracking
- **Order Processing** - Receive orders, manage fulfillment, send shipping notifications
- **Customer Accounts** - Optional customer login with order history and saved addresses
- **Discount Codes** - Create coupons with percentage or fixed discounts
- **Digital Products** - Sell downloadable files with secure delivery
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Track revenue, orders, and top products (Pro tier)
- **Custom Themes** - Personalize your store's appearance (Pro tier)
- **Custom Domain** - Use your own domain name (Pro tier)

## How It Works

1. **Configure Your Store** - Use our wizard to set up your store name, branding, and connect your Stripe account
2. **Choose Your Tier** - Select Starter, Pro, or Hosted based on your needs
3. **Deploy Instantly** - Your store goes live at `yourstore.gosovereign.io` in under 60 seconds
4. **Start Selling** - Add products, share your link, and start accepting payments
