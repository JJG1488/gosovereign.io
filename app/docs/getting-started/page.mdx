# Getting Started

This guide walks you through your first 15 minutes with GoSovereign - from purchase to your first sale.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [gosovereign.io/wizard](/wizard)
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a magic link (check spam folder if needed)
4. Click the link to verify your account

## Step 2: Configure Your Store

The setup wizard guides you through 8 quick steps:

### Store Basics
- **Store Name** - Your business name (e.g., "Mountain Gear Co.")
- **Tagline** - A short description (e.g., "Premium outdoor equipment")
- **Logo** - Upload your logo (optional, can add later)

### Branding
- **Brand Color** - Pick your primary color for buttons and accents
- **Description** - Tell customers what you sell

### Contact Info
- **Email** - Where customers reach you
- **Phone** - Optional but recommended
- **Address** - Your business location (shown in footer)

### Social Links
- Add links to your Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, etc.

### Connect Stripe
This is required to accept payments:

1. Click "Connect with Stripe"
2. Create a Stripe account or log in to existing
3. Complete Stripe's onboarding
4. You'll be redirected back to GoSovereign

> **Important:** Stripe sends your earnings directly to your bank account. GoSovereign never touches your money.

## Step 3: Choose Your Tier

Select the tier that fits your needs:

- **Starter ($149)** - Up to 10 products, basic themes
- **Pro ($299)** - Unlimited products, analytics, custom domain
- **Hosted ($149 + $19/mo)** - Everything managed for you

See [Pricing & Tiers](/docs/tiers) for a full comparison.

## Step 4: Deploy Your Store

After payment, click **"Deploy Now"** and your store will be live in under 60 seconds at:

```
https://your-store-name.gosovereign.io
```

## Step 5: Add Your First Product

1. Go to your store's admin: `your-store.gosovereign.io/admin`
2. Log in with your email (same one from setup)
3. Click **Products** in the sidebar
4. Click **Add Product**
5. Fill in:
   - Product name
   - Description
   - Price
   - Upload images
   - Set inventory (optional)
6. Click **Save**

Your product is now live on your store!

## Step 6: Share Your Store

Your store is ready to accept orders. Share your link:

- Post on social media
- Add to your website or bio
- Send to your email list

## Quick Tips

### Check Your Spam Folder
Magic link emails sometimes land in spam. Add `info@gosovereign.io` to your contacts.

### Test a Purchase
Before promoting your store, make a test purchase to ensure everything works. You can refund it through your Stripe dashboard.

### Mobile-Friendly
Your store and admin dashboard work on mobile devices. Manage orders from anywhere.

### Secure Payments
All payments are processed through Stripe. Your store uses HTTPS encryption automatically.
