## Starter - $149

**Best for:** Testing the waters, small product catalogs, side projects.

The Starter tier gives you everything you need to launch a simple store:

- Up to 10 products
- Full order management
- Customer accounts
- Coupon system
- Digital product delivery
- 3 basic theme presets
- Hosted at `yourstore.gosovereign.io`

**Limitations:**
- 10 product maximum
- No analytics dashboard
- No custom domain
- Basic themes only

## Pro - $299

**Best for:** Growing businesses, established brands, power users.

The Pro tier unlocks all features with no product limits:

- **Unlimited products** - No restrictions on catalog size
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Track revenue, orders, and top-selling products
- **Premium Themes** - 6 professionally designed color schemes
- **Custom Domain** - Use your own domain (e.g., `shop.yourbrand.com`)
- **Priority Support** - Faster response times

Pro is a one-time purchase. Pay once, use forever.

## Hosted - $149 + $19/month

**Best for:** Hands-off store owners who want managed infrastructure.

The Hosted tier includes everything in Pro, plus:

- **Managed Hosting** - We handle the infrastructure
- **Automatic Updates** - Get new features without redeploying
- **Daily Backups** - Your data is protected
- **SSL Certificates** - Automatic HTTPS renewal
- **Uptime Monitoring** - We keep your store online

The $149 setup fee covers initial configuration. The $19/month covers ongoing hosting and maintenance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade later?

Yes! You can upgrade from Starter to Pro at any time. You'll pay the difference in price, and your store will be redeployed with the new features unlocked.

### What happens if I cancel Hosted?

If you cancel your Hosted subscription, your store continues to work until the end of your billing period. After that, you can download your store as a ZIP file and self-host it.

### Do I own my store?

Absolutely. Every GoSovereign store can be downloaded as a complete Next.js application. You can self-host it anywhere - Vercel, Netlify, your own server. No lock-in.

### What payment methods are supported?

All stores use Stripe for payments. Stripe supports credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and more. You connect your own Stripe account and receive funds directly.

GoSovereign does not charge transaction fees. You only pay Stripe's standard processing fees (typically 2.9% + 30Â¢ per transaction).
